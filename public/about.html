About StockFx

StockFx is on a mission to make investing accessible and powerful for everyone. Founded by traders and engineers, we focus on speed, transparency, and security — delivering professional tools with zero complexity.

Our Mission

We believe investing should be simple, affordable, and available to everyone. We build powerful tools to give both retail and professional investors access to the markets with enterprise-grade infrastructure and a delightful user experience.

Team

Our team is a mix of former traders, engineers, designers, and regulatory experts. We operate with a customer-first mindset, and our engineering culture emphasizes reliability, observability, and low-latency systems.

Security & Compliance

Security is foundational: encryption in transit and at rest, strict access controls, SOC 2 practices, and regular audits and penetration testing. We partner with regulated entities for brokerage and custody services to ensure client funds and assets are handled safely.
